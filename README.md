 # 📊 Business Insights 360 | AtliQ Hardware

## 📊 Project Overview

**Business Insights 360** is an end-to-end Power BI solution developed for **AtliQ Hardware**, designed to provide a comprehensive 360° view of business performance across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain 

The dashboard empowers stakeholders to monitor key business KPIs, analyze revenue and profitability trends, evaluate customer and product performance, improve forecast accuracy and support data-driven decision-making through interactive and insightful visualizations.

This project demonstrates the application of advanced Power BI concepts, including data modeling, DAX calculations, Power Query transformations, KPI design and business analytics techniques to solve real-world business challenges.

The solution was developed by following and implementing concepts learned from the Codebasics Power BI Course.

## 🎯 Business Problem

AtliQ Hardware relied on multiple disconnected reports and spreadsheets for business analysis, making it difficult to gain a unified view of organizational performance.

The objective of this project was to build a centralized analytics solution that enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor key business KPIs
* Analyze profitability and revenue trends
* Identify growth opportunities across products, customers and regions
* Improve forecast accuracy and demand planning
* Track supply chain performance and inventory risks
* Support data-driven decision-making across Finance, Sales, Marketing and Executive teams
